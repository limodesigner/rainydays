Warm, stretchy and breathable like our Regulator® fleece, but with the added benefits of abrasion and weather resistance. This cool-weather crosslayer was designed for extended versatility in shifting mountain conditions. Fair Trade Certified™ sewn.

Our minimal, waterproof PFC-free jacket is designed for living on the road—the jacket you take to the boulders or crag on a grey sky day and keep stashed within reach. This hard shell meets our rigorous H2No™ Performance Standard requirements for waterproofness and durability and is built of NetPlus® 100% postconsumer recycled nylon plain-weave fabric made from recycled fishing nets to help reduce ocean plastic pollution, plus it's Fair Trade Certified™ sewn. Above all, the Slate Sky Jacket was designed to be multifunctional and simple.
